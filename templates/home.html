ABOUT US

WHAT WE DO?

We are here to assist India's elderly people so that they may live their lives quietly even after retirement, knowing that they have the right to a pension if they are qualified.

WHY WE DO?

We do this to guarantee that every family in India that is qualified for a pension but is not receiving one understands their rights and requests the pension they are entitled to.

HOW WE DO?

We get your information from the official government website, nsap.in, compare it to the data on the SECC website, and publish the findings clearly so you don't have to go anywhere else to get your name for pension.
